Picture of me, big hair and all. 


Starting this new blog is a huge deal to me. Mainly because I can blog and focus on my art at the same time. It's like a dream come true! Comics and stories are upfront, and the blog is a little hidden away.

I'm not planning on posting as much about making comics as I have in the past, but I whenever I find a helpful tip or technique, I will still share it.

In general, I will be sharing project status updates and some of the stuff I get involved in while not working on comic pages.

Speaking of updates, I've added Couture pages 5-8, and Mandy & Molly pages 4 & 5 will be up this Sunday. ^_^

So what do you think about my new site? Feel free to share your thoughts in the comments!
